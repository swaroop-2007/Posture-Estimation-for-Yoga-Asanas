About Us

Posture Estimation for Yoga

Recently yoga has been gaining lots of traction in India as well as in other countries. Yoga is known for being extremely beginner friendly and encourages all round development. It also acts as a mental health detox. Every year International Yoga Day is regligiously followed and tons of newcomers are exploring yoga.Thus we feel that these newcomers should have a helping hand in yoga. This thought led to the idea of using cutting edge ML tech to build a platform that will guide such people. We believe this platform will enourage people to practice yoga safely while also helping them improve in it.The basic idea behind this is creating a 3d skeletal structure which will mimic user's yoga asanas and also tell them how well they are doing. We plan to achieve this by using Keypoint_RCNN_FPN for the 2d keypoint generation and Dialated Temporal Convolutions to convert 2d keypoints to 3d. Then we aim to use a similarity function to esitmate how well the user is doing.
